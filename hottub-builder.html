Decision sheet

Four answers and this tool goes live. Everything else is already locked.

  1. Why is the tub £5,495 in one onboarding deal and £7,995 in another? Size, spec, or a mistake in the pack? Risk: Ollie is being onboarded on a document quoting two prices for one product. Whichever he reads first is what he'll use.
  2. What's the standalone price — no sauna in the order? Risk: three live buyers are blocked on this. It's the most-asked figure we don't have.
  3. Do bespoke shapes carry an upcharge? Flat fee, percentage, or POA? Risk: closers are offering bespoke shapes with no pricing rule behind them.
  4. What ships as standard, and what's an upgrade? Jets, LEDs, massage jets, steps, thermocover. Risk: we describe the same tub as fully-loaded in the brochure and stripped-back on calls.
